Gutters

I've always enjoyed heights, and I like to do work that makes a difference. Pairing these with a love of Stihl's BR 700 leaf blower (seriously, it's amazing) and the need to find work that fits in around taking classes, I started cleaning out gutters in the summer of 2018. Since then, I've had experience cleaning gutters and rooftops on numerous building types: residential homes, industrial buildings, entire apartment complexes, and everything in between. I have the necessary experience, ropes, and equipment for multi-story and other more difficult cleans.
